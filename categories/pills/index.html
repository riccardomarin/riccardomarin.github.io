A little journal without any ambition
I repent for not keeping a journal during my PhD. Annotating facts and thoughts is essential when dealing with complex processes. I do that for my research projects, but I do not for my academic experience. Curiously, I am also used to writing short tales but never related to my study path. I feel the urge to fill this gap. Apart from the sentimental perspective (I enjoy reading notes I took years before), this regret has practical reasons.
